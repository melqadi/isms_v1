assets/              (for public files) (should be public, place only index.php in here)
assets/css/
assets/images
assets/js            (for your js files, or custom generated ones)
lib/                      (for my libs)  (should be private)
lib/vendor/               (for 3rd party libs)
app/              (for the whole app) (should be private)
app/         (classes that make the app work such as mainApp, Controller, Model, View, etc...)
app/model   (all the models)
app/view    (all the views)
app/view/html (templates used by the views)
app/controller (all controllers)
app/helper  (helper functions)
app/lib     (libs that you develop for the app)
app/template      (layout and/or templates for the app)
app/conf          (config files)
app/log           (log files)
app/cron          (scheduled jobs)
app/database      (for database migration scripts)
